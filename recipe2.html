Strawberry Romanoff

Ingredients:

1. Strawberries - 2 pint

2. Sugar - 4 tbs

3. Grand Marnier - 4 tbs

4. Cream - 1 cup

5. Sour Cream - 1/4 cup

Instructions:

1. In a medium bowl, combine hulled and quartered strawberries, 4 Tbsp sugar and 4 Tbsp liqueur, stir to combine then cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour and up to 2 hours, stirring once or twice.

2. Just before serving, in a large mixing bowl, combine 1 cup cold heavy cream and 1/4 cup powdered sugar, and beat with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.

3. Using a spatula, fold in 1/4 cup sour cream just until well blended. To serve, stir strawberries then divide between 6 serving glasses or bowls.

4. You can spoon a little syrup over the berries if you like. You can also use this syrup to soak a cake. Spoon cream over strawberries, dividing evenly.

5. You can also use an ice cream scoop with trigger release for a nice rounded puff of cream. Serve right away or chill and enjoy within 2 hours of assembly.
